Biography

About the author

Alexandra Reid is a senior editor and fashion journalist whose work explores the converging worlds of artificial intelligence, sustainable production, and the human desire to dress with intention. Based in Los Angeles, she has spent eight years reporting for some of fashion's most respected editorial voices.

Before joining Nation, Alexandra served as a contributing editor at Vogue Business and The Business of Fashion, where her long-form investigations into supply chain transparency and material innovation earned wide recognition. Her 2022 investigation into greenwashing in the fast fashion sector was cited by three legislative bodies.

She approaches fashion not as a frivolous pursuit but as a profound cultural signal — a lens through which we understand identity, economics, technology, and the environment simultaneously. Her writing is known for making complex systems feel personal and urgent.
